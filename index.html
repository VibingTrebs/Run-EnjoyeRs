Estimated using the ACSM submaximal running equation combined with your heart rate reserve (Uth–Sørensen method). Each run where your HR is 40–97% of reserve contributes an estimate; we take the 25th–75th percentile median from your last 90 days to smooth out outliers. The number rises as your pace improves at the same heart rate.You're a beast, your VO₂ max is at an all time high!
